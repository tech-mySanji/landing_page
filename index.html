Fresh, Local, and Delivered to Your Doorstep – No Extra Cost

At MySanji, we are passionate about connecting you with the freshest fish, meat, and vegetables from your local markets. Our mission is simple: provide high-quality, fresh products from trusted local vendors, farmers, and suppliers right to your kitchen, without charging any additional fees.

About

We envision a world where local farmers and businesses thrive, fresh food is accessible to all, and community support strengthens local markets.

Fresh Market-to-Door Delivery

We bring the best of your local markets directly to your home. Whether it’s seafood, farm-fresh vegetables, or quality cuts of meat, you can trust us to deliver only the freshest goods. Best of all, you get to see and approve the products before accepting them!

Customer Satisfaction – Your Choice Matters

We understand how important quality is when it comes to your kitchen. That’s why we give you the option to inspect the goods in person at delivery. If you’re not satisfied, you can reject them without any hassle.

Empowering Local Businesses

We are on a mission to strengthen local communities by empowering small farmers and vendors. By connecting them directly with you, we help keep local businesses thriving while providing you with the freshest, locally sourced products.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know: Answers to your most common questions about us.

How does MySanji connect Local Farmers and Vendors to Their Customers?

We partner directly with farmers and local vendors to ensure their products reach your home in the freshest condition. By shortening the supply chain, we provide better value for both suppliers and customers while promoting sustainable practices.

How can I provide feedback or leave a review?

We welcome your feedback! You can leave a review on our website or send your comments to info@mysanji.com. Your input helps us improve our services and better serve you.

How do I place an order with MySanji?

Simply browse our selection of fresh produce, meat, and other essentials on our online platform, place your order, and we’ll handle the rest.

Are there any additional fees for delivery?

No, we do not charge any extra fees for delivering fresh, local products to your home. What you see is what you pay—there are no hidden charges.

Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?

If you need to modify or cancel your order, please contact us as soon as possible. We will accommodate changes based on the status of your order and our delivery schedule.

How do you support local farmers and vendors?

By partnering directly with local farmers and vendors, we help them reach a broader audience, supporting their businesses and strengthening the local economy.
